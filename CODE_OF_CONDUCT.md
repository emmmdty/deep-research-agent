# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We want this repository to be a respectful, useful place for learning and collaboration.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful in technical discussion
- Giving actionable, specific feedback
- Assuming good intent while still being rigorous
- Welcoming people with different backgrounds and experience levels

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Bad-faith arguments or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Spam, trolling, or abusive language

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors whose behavior harms the project or its community.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer through GitHub and include enough context for review.
