# Observability and Tracing

Public observability materials make traces, spans, and diagnostic visibility explicit parts of AI system operation rather than hidden implementation details.[1][2]

At the same time, the public materials leave some uncertainty about how much observability platforms own evaluation workflows versus simply exposing telemetry for other systems to interpret.[1][2]

Claim Preview

  • supported: Public observability materials consistently position tracing and diagnostic visibility as a separate operational layer.
  • supported: Public observability materials do not fully settle whether evaluation ownership belongs inside observability tooling or in adjacent systems.
